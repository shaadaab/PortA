My Work

Journalism Internship - Bengaluru Post

During my four-week internship at Bengaluru Post, I researched, reported, and presented stories on urban infrastructure, environmental sustainability, governance, and community events. I wrote articles on issues like BMTC buses, greywater treatment, and deforestation, covered events such as a literature festival and vegan food fair, and conducted field interviews. I also provided voiceovers for news videos and did on-camera reporting on civic issues. A key highlight was my RT Nagar EV accident report, which prompted BBMP action. This experience sharpened my skills in research, writing, and multimedia journalism while offering valuable insights into India’s dynamic media landscape and the role of journalism in driving civic impact.
